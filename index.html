Why I Created Pip

I'm a teacher with an International Elementary Education degree and a parent of a 7-year-old. One day I noticed my daughter watching videos online and not learning anything from them. So I built something different. Pip's Discovery Lab teaches real NGSS-aligned science in a way kids actually stay engaged with. Every episode is something I would show in my own classroom.

Episode 01
The Mystery of the Magnetic Cat
NGSS 3-PS2-3 & 3-PS2-4 Grade 3 · Ages 8–9

Pip tries to build a magnetic cat toy — but the soda can won't stick to his magnet. When Whiskers's metal collar tag gets pulled toward the magnet, the mystery begins. A fun, science-verified episode about magnets, magnetic fields, and why only some metals are magnetic.

Students will learn

What a magnetic field is and how it works
The three ferromagnetic metals (Fe, Ni, Co)
Why some metals are magnetic and others aren't
How opposite poles attract and same poles repel
Memory trick: "I Need Chocolate" (Iron, Nickel, Cobalt)
How magnetic fields pass through non-magnetic materials
